# World's Largest Civic Action: World Cleanup Day!

Over 17 million trash heroes have already cleaned up more than 500 000 tonnes of trash in over 100 countries! üòÉ 

The movement against pollution is growing fast and we're now building the World Cleanup App and Platform for the hundreds of millions of people people joining the World Cleanup Day on **15th of September 2018**.
